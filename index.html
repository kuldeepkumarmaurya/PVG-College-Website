About college

We pleased that you are considering Pune Vidyarthi Griha's College of Science & Technology for next step in your career. We would like to warmly welcome you. This B.Sc. Computer & Information Technology Degree has a very good reputation as a prouder of training and educating young people interested in making a career in Lab based industries. Bachelor of Mass Media and Bachelor of Management Studies give education to students in Management and Media Field.These degrees offered here are designed to meet the increasing needs of industry and the demand for careers with a specialized and practical needs.The institute in future plans to start Engineering courses. Throughout the academic year we provide opportunities for students to participate in many activities and urge parents to stay closely involved in the education of their children. Our teaching staff have extensive experience and strong link with the industries. these teachers with the excellent facilities at the college help to ensure that our courses are practical, relevant and will lead to good opportunities for employment. Education is a life long process, professional education in general and B.Sc.
